Our mission: Change the world by spreading kindness and fellowship among people.


Loving generosity. Helping others without purposes

For most people, "Just be a nice person," seems like an easily attainable goal. Yet is a distinguish gap between simply helping someone, an act of kindness and true love of beeing generous. Many people regard generosity as an obligation, a kind of need as human beeing. Sometimes they would feel relief if the charity collector happens to bypass their door. It's true that they would give if asked, and the action would be deemed a genorosity, yet their attitude falls far short of the ideal. With the goal of helping every one develop a positive mindset toward real generosity, realizing its importance in human relationships. Beeing generous and kind - doing is no less than an emulation of G-dly compassion, It links all the world and underlies the relationship with all Creation.

How can we make this effectively?

The big change is to be willing to help others. But how to help? What can I do for someone else?
Here comes HELP! We connect people who need help to people who want to help.
To make it easier, we divided into groups the subjects where people who need help can be found by those who want to help:

  • Helping in the road: Here people can visualize about help with everything that is related to transportation, such as: ride, car repairs etc.
  • Helping in the home: Did you ever need help to fix your sink? And what about cleaning tips? Here you find and ask about these stuff.
  • Ask for help: Fill a form discribing how can we help!
  • Offer Help: Fill a form to offer help to others.
  • How can I help? Posts, links and other stuff to help you day by day.
